Citing Arvest

Arvest is a tool that emerges from a 15-year research project led by Clarisse Bardiot that investigates tools, digital traces and the performing arts. Most recently, Arvest emerges from Bardiot's ERC-funded Project STAGE. If you wish to cite the tool in your scholarly writing, please use one of the following references.

Journal of Digital History article

Our article In search of an interpretative environment for digital traces: the building of Arvest gives a full account of the development of Arvest, both from technical and from a theoretical perspectives.

Link
      Bardiot, Clarisse, Jacob Hart, and David Rouquet. “In Search of an Interpretative Environment for Digital Traces: The Building of Arvest.” Sec. Jdh003. Journal of Digital History, no. jdh003 (June 2024). https://journalofdigitalhistory.org/en/article/8X2RX68Qavxr.
